# Kindergarten, 2019-2020
Our Family's Kindergarten Plan. 
Buy any books used where possible.

The curriculum book list can be found [here](https://github.com/bekabug/homeschool-kindergarten/blob/master/booklist.md) and the literature list can be found [here](https://github.com/bekabug/homeschool-kindergarten/blob/master/literature.md)

## Overview
* 240 minutes a day
* 180 days
* Beginning August 8, 2019

## The Subjects

### Language Arts
* Phonics
* Sight words
* Spanish
* Writing
* Spelling
* Poetry
* Storytelling
* Journaling

### Social Studies
* Geography & Maps
* Culture & Civilizations
* The USA
* Community
* Good Citizenship
* What do people do all day?

### Science
* Kumon Science
* Electronics
* Science experiments
* Weather

### Math
* Adding
* Subtracting
* Shapes
* Telling time
* Money

### Movement
* Tee ball/baseball machine
* Yoga
* Free play
* Gardening
* Gymansium
* Museum

### Health
* Anatomy
* Emotions & feelings
* Manners
* Meditation/Mindfulness

### Arts & Crafts
* Origami
* Coloring
* Tracing
* Drawing
* Painting
* Clay
* Stop motion video

### Music
* Singing
* Ocarina/Recorder
* Ukulele
* Piano
* Drum
* Listening & Appreciating
